Warm

The project Warm, was the start and concept of a game created by some friends and I during the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program. The game was a way to promote mental health awareness in a way where people can get a better understanding. Currently, the game is unfinished and only has a general outline/concept through scratch and several other pieces of paper. In the future, I plan to work on the game further as well as finish polishing the website for it as well.
