Junior Reserve Officers Training Corps (2016 - Present)


Staff 6 Technology and Communications


In her staff, she currently holds the positiion of Non-Commissioned Officer in Command Senior (NCOIC). Some of the various tasks that the staff does is set up for a multitude of events. Some of the events we go to the sound booth in our school and sets up videos to preview, projectors, mixers, microphones, walkie talkies, audio, and lighting. Other events are similar set ups but more portable by not using the auditorium speakers and setting up different types of amplifiers. Other duties include maintaining the website for our Patriot Battlion and fixing and maintaining the quality of the equipment that JROTC owns such as desktops, laptops, printers, copiers, televisions, and any other appliances listed above. Moreover, we handle carving nameplates with a gravograph for all of the cadets in our battalion. What we do to make nameplates is a long process through several computer applications as well as coordinating all of our lists with the list of cadets that our army instructors give them. Mostly we get lists of last names to be made to get engraved on the nameplates; then making models for them in AutoDesk ArtCam.
